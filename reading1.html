I think restaurant websites are a great way for restaurants to express their unique personality and atmosphere, while also serving as a centralized hub for finding information. Not only can you get a good sense of what the restaurant experience might feel like from a well-designed website, but you can also learn more about the restaurant's story, menu items, locations, etc.

Having used website building tools like SquareSpace and Wix, it's interesting to think about the trade-off between templates and creative freedom. While these platforms make it easy to change things like fonts and colors, sometimes templates can feel a bit constraining as you can't always customize every single detail.

I'm curious to see how new technologies like generative AI will impact user's ability to create websites. I feel like it could help make web-design more accessible to non-technical users, but I wonder how it will impact creativity and personalization.
