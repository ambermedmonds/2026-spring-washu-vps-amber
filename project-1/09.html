NUDO HOUSE

Nudo House is a fast-casual, pan-asian noodle house specializing in ramen, pho, banh mi, appetizers, soft serve and more. Ultimately, Nudo is the celebration of how powerful food can be fueled by that gratitude and inspiration for its ability to bring people together and make everyone feel at home anywhere, anytime.

LOCATIONS

We currently have two locations in St. Louis - Creve Coeur and The Delmar Loop.
