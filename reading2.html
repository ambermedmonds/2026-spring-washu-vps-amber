One quote that stood out to me in this reading was "from someone, for someone, in a particular context." This rubric shifted my approach on how to evaluate whether a piece of work was created by a human or AI. The biggest takeaway for me is the importance of expressiveness, emotion resonance, and being able to sense that a real human is sharing their own experience and perspective. I think this rubric criteria will become more and more critical to keep in mind, especially as AI continues to improve going forward.
